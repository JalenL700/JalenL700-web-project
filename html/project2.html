Hajime no Ippo!

This webpage is about my favorite Manga/Anime.

Hajime no ippo is a Japanese made manga and anime exploring the boxing journey of Ippo Makanouchi. From his beginning as a bullied high school boy to a professional boxer.

This show has multiple series, which are listed below.

  1. Hajime no Ippo the fighting
  2. Hajime no Ippo new challenger
  3. Hajime no Ippo Rising
  4. Hajime no Ippo Mashibe vs Kimura

The series was so influential that in Toyko, where the show takes place. There is a boxing gym inspired by the anime, with a statue of Takemura on the roof, another character in the show that helps Ippo along his boxing career.
